Boondi Raita

Description

This a spicy yogurt side from India made with curd and boondi.

Ingredients

  • Curd.
  • Water.
  • Salt to taste.
  • Chaat masala .
  • Cumin powder.
  • Red chilli powder
  • Boondi.

Steps

  1. In a bowl add curd.
  2. Add water to improve consistency.
  3. Add red chilli, cumin, chaat masala powder.
  4. Add boondi and mix well.
  5. Ready .
