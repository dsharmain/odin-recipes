Kadhi Daal

Description

This a healthy dish from India made with Moong and Masoor daal.

Ingredients

  • Half cup peele/moong daal.
  • Half cup masoor daal.
  • 8 cup water.
  • One small Onion chopped.
  • Cilantro chopped.
  • Garam masala two spoons.
  • Half spoon red mirchi powder
  • One spoon Salt.
  • One spoon Haldi.
  • Two spoons of oil

Steps

  1. Clean the dal with water.
  2. Put the daal in the kadhai and water and let it come to a boil under medium heat.
  3. Add Salt and haldi to the daal while it starts to heat up.
  4. Sauté the onions in the oil until golden brown.
  5. Add garam masala and mirchi powder and cook for half minute.
  6. Add cilantro in the end.
  7. Once the daal is done in about 30 mins add the sauté mixture to it.
  8. Your Daal is ready to be served.
