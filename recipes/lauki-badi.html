Lauki Badi

Description

This a healthy dish from India made with Lauki and Badi.

There is a video as well which is slightly different style of making this curry which you can watch on youtube link below.

Youtube Lauki Badi

I have provided below my moms recipe. Thats how I make it.

Ingredients

  • Lauki half diced.
  • One Tomato chopped.
  • Cumin seeds 2 tsp
  • Salt to taste.
  • Badi one broken to pieces.
  • Turmeric powder.
  • Red chilli powder
  • Garam masala half tbsp.
  • Oil 2 tbsp.
  • 2 green chilies
  • 1 inch ginger

Steps

  1. In a cooker add oil and let it heat.
  2. Add Badi. Let it roast and remove it.
  3. Add Cumin seeds.
  4. Add ginger and chilies.
  5. Add tomatoes. Sauté them for sometime.
  6. Add haldi.
  7. Add red chilli powder.
  8. Add salt.
  9. Add the Lauki and sauté the mixture for couple minutes.
  10. Add the roasted badi.
  11. Add water and cover the cooker.
  12. Wait for one whistle. Once the pressure is released. Open the cover and cook so the excess water is evaporated.
  13. Add garam masala and cook 2 minutes.
  14. Garnish with some coriander. Ready to be served.
